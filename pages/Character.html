Kenny Johnson

Kenny, endowed with electric powers, leads a group called The Chosen Ones. He is kind and strong but struggles with arrogance. Seeking redemption for his past immaturity, Kenny decides to live for Jesus and use his powers for good, aiming to stop the Dark Figure and others who misuse their abilities. He faces a strained relationship with his wealthy parents and is learning to shed his arrogance and become a Godly man with the support of his team.

Mark Grover

Mark possesses environmental-adaptability powers, granting him different abilities based on his surroundings. Despite his involvement with a gang of street thugs, a situation forced upon him, he longs to leave and care for his family. The gang offers easy money, and the intimidating leader, Deshawn Harris, keeps Mark in line. However, The Chosen Ones offer Mark a chance to escape the gang and turn his life around if he genuinely desires it.

Zachary Morales

Zachary has teleportation and precognition powers and is Dominican. He uses his powers for good after seeing his brother, Brendan, use his powers for evil. He joins The Chosen Ones to stop Brendan and the Dark Figure and take care of his mother. He’s also seen a vision that can literally save the world.

Lorena Alvarez

Lorena has light powers and is Puerto Rican. She is beautiful and walks with grace. She is dating Andres Dominguez, who she thinks is perfect, which makes her think she is unworthy to date him. Lorena also seems to have a personal connection with the Dark Figure since her powers counteract his. She’s determined to discover who this Dark Figure is.

Benjamin Graham

Benjamin, endowed with telepathy and telekinesis, serves as the older brother and guardian of Jeremy Graham. Having fled home as a young teen, convinced his parents were murdered by the government, he recently learned that his mother is alive and is now determined to find her. Alongside this quest, Benjamin is training Jeremy to master his own powers.

Jeremy Graham

Jeremy, the younger brother of Benjamin Graham, possesses telepathy and telekinesis. Still in high school and struggling to control his newly-acquired abilities, Jeremy is encouraged by Benjamin to live as normal a life as possible. As a result, he typically stays home when he's not at school or training. However, the Dark Figure seems to have plans to use Jeremy for his own purposes.

Ashley Reese

Ashley possesses fire powers and is also a skilled martial artist and gymnast. With a no-nonsense attitude, she deeply cares for her family and friends, always striving to please them. After fleeing home due to a fiery accident, Ashley became entangled with a cult led by a dark magician. She later joins The Chosen Ones to defeat him and learn how to incinerate evil.
